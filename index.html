Who We Are

About Pharos

We are a new theater company housed at the Pickens County Performing Arts Center in Liberty, SC — focused on bringing high-quality, family-friendly live theater to our community.

Pharos (fah-ros) means lighthouse. We chose this name because we want to shine a light on Liberty, SC. We have many talented performers in our community, and we want to provide opportunities for Liberty to shine.

From gripping dramas to beloved children's classics, each production is an opportunity for performers of all ages to share their gifts — and for audiences to experience the magic of live theater together.

"To enrich our community by providing opportunities for all ages to participate in high-quality live theater productions, offering educational opportunities for youth and children in the performing arts, and producing family-friendly entertainment for the community and its visitors."
— OUR MISSION
